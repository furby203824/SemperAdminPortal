## Scope

MARADMIN 184/26 announces the Navy Flight Demonstration Squadron (Blue Angels) is accepting enlisted Marine applications. The MARADMIN supports the FY26 Blue Angels selection board referenced in MARADMIN 224/26. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Active component enlisted Marines interested in Blue Angels assignment. Apply per the solicitation criteria.
- Career planners. Process applications.
- HQMC MMEA. Coordinates with the Blue Angels for selection.

## Action items

1. Pull the application package requirements through the unit Career Counselor.
2. Confirm obligated service through TFRS.
3. Submit through the MOL EPAR module.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 224/26. CY2026 SNCO Selection Boards. References this MARADMIN as the application solicitation.

## Status

Released 2026-04. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MM. Applicability is the Active Component.
