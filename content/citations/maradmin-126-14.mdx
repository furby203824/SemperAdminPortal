## Scope

MARADMIN 126/14 announced the reorganization of Manpower Management (MM) Division at Headquarters Marine Corps. The reorganization renamed Manpower Management Integration Administration (MMIA) under Manpower Management Integration Branch (MMIB) and renamed MMIA as MMIB-3. MMIB-3 became the Secretarial Process authority for CMC on all BAH-related waivers under JTR. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines submitting BAH waiver requests. Route through the chain of command to MMIB-3.
- HQMC Manpower Management. Owns the new structure.
- S-1 staff and adjutants. Apply the updated routing.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 7220.56A. Cites this MARADMIN for the MMIB-3 renaming.
- ALMAR 287/98. Original Secretarial Process authority establishment.

## Status

Released 2014-03. Originator CMC Washington DC. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force. Active. MMIB-3 continues as the BAH waiver Secretarial Process authority.
