## Scope

MARADMIN 270/05 announced the Aviation Commanders Preparation Program. The program prepared aviation officers for command of aviation units. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Aviation officers selected for command. Required attendance.
- HQMC Aviation. Owns the program.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P3500.72. Aviation Training and Readiness Program. Pairs with this MARADMIN.

## Status

Released 2005-06. Originator CMC Washington DC Aviation. Active during the listed program window. Verify against current Aviation Commanders Preparation Program guidance before applying.
