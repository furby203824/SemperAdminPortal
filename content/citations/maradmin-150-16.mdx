## Scope

MARADMIN 150/16 sets the quarterly Page 11 counseling requirement for Marines NOT RECOMMENDED for promotion to Corporal and Sergeant. The MARADMIN aligns counseling cadence with the JEPES promotion cycle. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Commanding officers. Conduct quarterly Page 11 counseling.
- S-1 staff. Track Page 11 entries.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P1400.32D. Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions.
- MCO P1070.12K. Marine Corps Individual Records Administration Manual (IRAM). Page 11 framework.
- MARADMIN 559/23. Junior Enlisted Remedial Promotions Automation. References Page 11 counseling.
