## Scope

MARADMIN 630/24 publishes updated Enlisted Professional Military Education (EPME) requirements for Active Duty and Active Reserve Marines. The MARADMIN sets the resident and non-resident course completion requirements by grade. Applicability is the Active Component and Active Reserve.

## Audience

- Active Duty and Active Reserve enlisted Marines. Apply EPME requirements by grade.
- Career planners. Track EPME completion for promotion eligibility.
- HQMC TECOM. Owns the requirements.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 1553.4B. Professional Military Education (PME).
- MARADMIN 474/21. Earlier EPME Requirements by Grade. Predecessor framework.
- MARADMIN 631/24. Updated EPME Requirements for SMCR. Companion Reserve Component message.
- MARADMIN 222/26. Sergeant TIS and Staff Sergeant Promotion Process. Cross-references EPME for sequenced PME requirement.

Released 2024-12. Originator CG TECOM Quantico VA. Applicability is the Active Component and Active Reserve.
