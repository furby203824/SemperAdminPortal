## Scope

MARADMIN 474/23 provided advance notification of upcoming changes to Special Leave Accrual (SLA) under MCO 1050.3J Regulations for Leave, Liberty, and Administrative Absence. The MARADMIN was superseded by MARADMIN 188/25 which set the 90-day accrued leave ceiling (60 days annual leave plus 30 days SLA carryover from one FY to the next).

## Audience

- Active Duty and Active Reserve Marines. The current SLA framework lives in MARADMIN 188/25.
- HQMC Manpower Plans and Policy (MPO). Owned the policy.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 188/25. Advance Notification of Changes to Special Leave Accrual. Cancels this MARADMIN and sets the current 90-day accrued leave ceiling.
- MCO 1050.3J. Regulations for Leave, Liberty, and Administrative Absence.

## Status

Released 2023-09. Cancelled by MARADMIN 188/25 dated 2025-04-11. Use this entry for historical context only.
