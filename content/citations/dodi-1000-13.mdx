## Scope

DODI 1000.13 establishes policy, assigns responsibilities, and provides procedures for issuing DoD identification cards. The instruction governs ID-card eligibility, issuance, and use across the Department. The instruction reissues the prior DODI 1000.13 and incorporates a Change 1 effective 14 December 2017.

## Audience

Real-Time Automated Personnel Identification System (RAPIDS) operators at every Marine Corps and DoD ID card facility. S-1 and IPAC personnel coordinating dependent enrollment. Marines, dependents, retirees, and other eligible communities receiving ID cards. Commanders accountable for ID-card accountability.

## Policy posture

- A distinct DoD ID card is issued to uniformed service members, their dependents, and other eligible individuals as proof of identity and DoD affiliation.
- DoD ID cards serve as the Geneva Convention Card for eligible personnel per DODI 1000.01.
- DoD ID cards are issued through a secure and authoritative process.
- The Common Access Card (CAC), a form of DoD ID card, serves as the Federal Personal Identity Verification (PIV) card for DoD implementation of HSPD-12.
- ID cards in a form distinct from the CAC issue for communities that do not require the Federal PIV card.

## Applicability

OSD, the Military Departments (including the Coast Guard at all times, including when in the Department of Homeland Security by agreement), the Joint Chiefs of Staff and the Joint Staff, the Combatant Commands, and the rest of the DoD enterprise.

## Status

Active. Reissues the prior DODI 1000.13. Incorporates Change 1 effective 14 December 2017. Approved by USD(P&R).
