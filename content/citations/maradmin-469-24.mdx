## Scope

MARADMIN 469/24 announces FY25 Intended Military Occupational Specialty (IMOS) promotions for the Active Component. The MARADMIN allows promotion in an intended (training) PMOS rather than the current PMOS for Marines transitioning between occupational fields. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Marines selected for promotion under an IMOS. Apply the IMOS promotion procedure.
- Career planners. Process IMOS promotions through MCTFS.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P1400.32D. Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions.
- MARADMIN 515/25. Non-Competitive IMOS Promotions. Successor framework.

Released 2024-10. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MM. Applicability is the Active Component.
