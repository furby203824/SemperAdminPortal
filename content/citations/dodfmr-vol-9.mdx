## Scope

Volume 9 of DOD Financial Management Regulation 7000.14-R sets travel policy for the Department of Defense. The volume governs temporary duty (TDY), permanent duty travel (PCS), transportation allowances, evacuation allowances, travel advances, and the processing of travel claims. Every TDY voucher and PCS claim a Marine submits flows through the rules in this volume.

## Audience

Every Marine who travels on orders. Disbursing clerks, travel pay clerks, and S-4 personnel preparing and processing claims. Authorizing Officials, Approving Officials, and Certifying Officials in DTS. Commanders making travel authorization decisions and managing travel-incurred debt for their unit. The order at the Marine Corps level is MCO 4650.39A.

## Authority

Office of the Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller) publishes the FMR. Each Volume 9 chapter updates on its own cadence. Substantive revisions are flagged with an asterisk preceding the section, paragraph, table, or figure that carries the change. Hyperlinks inside the regulation use bold italic blue text.

## Chapter map

- Chapter 1. General Information. June 2025. Frames the scope, authorities, and definitions framework for the volume.
- Chapter 2. Travel of Civilian Employees (covered in volume but not enclosed in this seed).
- Chapter 3. Hardship Duty and Other Allowances (covered in volume but not enclosed in this seed).
- Chapter 4. Transportation Allowances. August 2024. Rules for transportation costs incurred during official travel.
- Chapter 5. Temporary Duty Travel (TDY) and Travel Advances. January 2026. TDY entitlements, per diem, allowable expenses, and the advance-pay framework.
- Chapter 6. Permanent Duty Travel. May 2025. PCS entitlements including dislocation allowance, advance travel pay, and household goods.
- Chapter 7. Evacuation Allowances. February 2025. Evacuation pay and allowances for service members and dependents.
- Chapter 8. Processing Travel Claims. April 2024. Voucher routing, certifying officer responsibilities, debt collection, and audit posture.
- Definitions. September 2025. Defined terms used across the volume.
