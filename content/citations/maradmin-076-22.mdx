## Scope

MARADMIN 076/22 publishes updated requirements regarding implementation of Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance (SGLI). The MARADMIN aligns Marine Corps SGLI handling with current Department of the Navy and Veterans Affairs guidance. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines on Active Duty and in the Reserve Component. Track SGLI election and beneficiary designation.
- S-1 staff and IPACs. Process SGLI elections through MOL and OMPF updates.
- Casualty Assistance Calls Officers (CACOs). Reference SGLI status during casualty support.

## Connection to higher policy

- 38 U.S.C. Chapter 19. SGLI authority.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 3040.4. Marine Corps Casualty Assistance Program.

## Status

Released 2022-02. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.
