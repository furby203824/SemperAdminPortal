## When To Start

Section 1 actions begin no later than D-90, with the DEOCS initiation. Major signature events occur at D-15 (final Supply inventory), D-10 (SOCL submission), D-3 (Status of Command Brief), and D-0 (turnover ceremony).

Treat the timeline as fixed. CMC White Letter 3-17 is explicit on the SOCL and Brief deadlines. Slipping them is a direct deviation from HQMC guidance and is visible to the Reporting Senior.

## A. Pre-Departure Administrative Requirements

Required actions before the brief and before the ceremony.

| Action | Authority |
|--------|-----------|
| Attend Cornerstone (Commandant's Combined Commandership Course). Confirm incoming CO has attended before slating. | Annual Cornerstone MARADMIN (FY26: MARADMIN 388/25<Citation source="MARADMIN 388/25" />) |
| Submit Status of Command Letter (SOCL) to Reporting Senior NLT 10 days prior to relief. | CMC White Letter 3-17 |
| Conduct Status of Command Brief to RS or representative NLT 3 days prior to CoC. Covers operational readiness, material readiness, personnel readiness, training plan, 6-month focus, unresolved issues, assistance required. | CMC White Letter 3-17 |
| Initiate DEOCS within 90 days prior to relinquishing command. | MARADMIN 291/19<Citation source="MARADMIN 291/19" />; MARADMIN 468/23<Citation source="MARADMIN 468/23" /> |
| D-30: Direct all Responsible Officers to conduct physical inventory and reconcile with GCSS-MC. | 2016 Commanders Materiel Readiness Handbook |
| D-15: Direct Supply Officer to conduct full inventory before furnishing Certificate of Relief. | MCO 4400.201<Citation source="MCO 4400.201" /> Vol. 3 |
| Certify all staff section desktop procedures and turnover binders are current (S-1 through S-6 plus all Special Staff billets). | MCO 4400.201<Citation source="MCO 4400.201" /> Vol. 3 |
| Ensure CIRRAS warm handoff is complete for ALL Marines. Profiles fully updated before any Marine departs the unit. | MCO 1500.60A<Citation source="MCO 1500.60A" /> Para 4.b.(3)(g) |

The 10-day SOCL deadline is a Reporting Senior expectation and a HQMC guidance requirement. Do not submit the SOCL the day before the brief and treat it as compliant.

## B. Supply and Accountability Turnover

The Certificate of Relief is the operative document. It binds your accountability for everything held by the command at the moment of relief.

| Action | Authority |
|--------|-----------|
| Sign Certificate of Relief covering unit-held account accountability, fiscal status (budget execution and 2-quarter FY projection), and all open FLIPLs (DD Form 200<Citation source="DD Form 200" />) with FLO name, dollar value, and clock date. | MCO 4400.201<Citation source="MCO 4400.201" /> Vol. 3, Appendix G/H; CMC White Letter 3-17 |
| Close out and sign all adjudicated FLIPLs (DD Form 200<Citation source="DD Form 200" />) within your approval authority before relief. | MCO 4400.201<Citation source="MCO 4400.201" /> Vol. 17 |
| AA&E: 100 percent serialized inventory by serial number, lot, and DODIC reconciled. Provide TAMIS ammunition plan status. | MCO 5530.14A<Citation source="MCO 5530.14A" />; MCO 8023.3<Citation source="MCO 8023.3" />; MCO 5100.29C<Citation source="MCO 5100.29C" /> Vol. 8 |
| Sign DD Form 577<Citation source="DD Form 577" /> terminating all existing fiscal appointments: Supply Officer/APO, certifying officers, Disbursing Officer, GPC APC, fund holders, and DTS Authorizing Official. Incoming CO re-appoints all via new DD Form 577<Citation source="DD Form 577" />. | DoD FMR Vol. 5<Citation source="DoD FMR Vol. 5" /> Ch. 5; MCO 4400.201<Citation source="MCO 4400.201" /> Vol. 1; DoD FMR Vol. 9<Citation source="DoD FMR Vol. 9" /> |
| Brief GCSS-MC Unit User Account Manager (UUAM) assignments and personnel scheduled for GCSS Accountable Officer training. | MCO 4400.201<Citation source="MCO 4400.201" /> Vol. 1 |
| Address equipment currently deployed, temp loaned, or left behind: who has it, where, and duration. | Certificate of Relief enclosure; I MEFO 4355.1 concept guide |

A FLIPL within your authority that you fail to close before relief becomes the incoming CO's problem. The clock date does not pause for CoC.

## C. Military Justice Turnover Including OSTC

OSTC is the highest-leverage line on this section. Once OSTC exercises authority on a covered offense, neither the outgoing nor the incoming CO can dispose of the case.

| Action | Authority |
|--------|-----------|
| Identify ALL OSTC covered-offense cases by name. Brief incoming CO with STC POC, case status, and legal-hold requirements. Marines under OSTC case authority may NOT transfer, retire, separate, or re-enlist without OSTC and SJA coordination. | MARADMIN 633/24<Citation source="MARADMIN 633/24" />; JAGINST 5800.7G<Citation source="JAGINST 5800.7G" /> CH-2 |
| UPB brief: confirm all records current using NAVMC 10132<Citation source="NAVMC 10132" /> (Rev. 08-2023). Calendar-year organization. 3-year retention. | MCO 5800.16<Citation source="MCO 5800.16" /> Vol. 14; NAVMC 10132<Citation source="NAVMC 10132" /> (Rev. 08-2023) |
| Brief all open NJP cases, courts-martial in progress, promotion delays and withholds, pretrial confinement, and active Military Protective Orders. | MCO 5800.16<Citation source="MCO 5800.16" /> Vol. 12; JAGINST 5800.7G<Citation source="JAGINST 5800.7G" /> |
| Close out all courts-martial pending your signature as convening authority. Nothing transfers unsigned. | RCM 504; MCO 5800.16<Citation source="MCO 5800.16" /> Vol. 12 |
| Brief VWAP status: legal personnel, projected shortfalls, training deficiencies. | MCO 5800.14<Citation source="MCO 5800.14" />; IGMC FAC #38 |
| Summarize awards issued, outstanding award recommendations, and MSC/MSE award policy timeline. | SECNAVINST 1650.1J<Citation source="SECNAVINST 1650.1J" />; iAPS; I MEFO 4355.1 concept guide |

The OSTC list is documented in detail on the OSTC Covered Offenses page in this section. Use that page as the reference for what counts as a covered offense.

## D. Readiness and Special Programs Turnover

The Readiness Officer Turnover Binder is the operative document. It captures 12 months of readiness records and is required.

| Action | Authority |
|--------|-----------|
| Brief current DRRS-MC C/P/S/R/T-Level and rationale for subjective overrides. Deliver Readiness Officer Turnover Binder (12 months of records, IGMC FAC-3000, appointment letters, DRRS-MC training certificates). | MCO 3000.13B<Citation source="MCO 3000.13B" />; Commanders Readiness Handbook |
| SAPR: provide VA/SARC appointment letters, D-SAACP certifications, all open CMG cases, and any active Expedited Transfers. | MCO 1752.5C<Citation source="MCO 1752.5C" />; SAPR Smart Pack; DoDI 6495.02<Citation source="DoDI 6495.02" /> |
| FPC: brief ALL active CIRRAS-tracked Marines and risk determinations. Ensure warm handoff fully documented in CIRRAS before CoC. | MCO 1500.60A<Citation source="MCO 1500.60A" /> Para 4.b.(3)(g) |
| Brief all WII Marines: duty limitation codes, IDES cases, Recovery Care Coordinator linkages. | Wounded Warrior Leaders Guide; MCO 1900.16<Citation source="MCO 1900.16" /> CH-3 |
| Provide retention program status: reenlistment mission, boatspaces, FTAP/STAP submissions, CRP selectees. | MMEA Commanders Retention Toolkit |
| Brief BCP status, medical and dental readiness, ISOPREP status, and MICP Annual Certification Statement timeline. | I MEFO 4355.1 concept guide |

CIRRAS warm handoff is the highest-frequency failure on this section. Outgoing COs habitually treat the FPC briefing as the warm handoff. The MCO 1500.60A<Citation source="MCO 1500.60A" /> standard is documentation in CIRRAS, not a verbal brief.

## Common Failures on Section 1

1. **Late SOCL.** Submitted within a week of relief instead of 10 days prior. Reporting Senior visibility on the deviation.
2. **Certificate of Relief signed without final inventory.** Outgoing CO signs based on the prior monthly count instead of the D-15 inventory. Inventory must be current.
3. **Open FLIPLs left for incoming CO.** Adjudicated FLIPLs within outgoing authority must close before relief. Failing this transfers the action to incoming CO without authority adjustment.
4. **OSTC case identification by category instead of by name.** Briefing "we have three covered offenses" is insufficient. Each case briefed by name with STC POC.
5. **DD Form 577<Citation source="DD Form 577" /> sweep deferred to incoming.** All predecessor appointments terminate at CoC. Outgoing CO terminates. Incoming CO re-appoints. The two actions are separate.

## Sign-Off Checklist

Before the ceremony, confirm in writing:

1. SOCL submitted to RS NLT 10 days prior - date and addressee
2. Status of Command Brief delivered NLT 3 days prior - date and audience
3. DEOCS initiated NLT 90 days prior - date and reference
4. Certificate of Relief signed - based on D-15 inventory
5. DD Form 577<Citation source="DD Form 577" /> sweep complete - all fiscal appointments terminated
6. OSTC case list briefed by name to incoming CO with STC POC
7. CIRRAS warm handoff documented for every high-risk Marine
8. Readiness Officer Turnover Binder delivered

If any item is incomplete, do not sign the joint certification block.
