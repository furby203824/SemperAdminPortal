## Scope

MARADMIN 412/23 announces the transition from the Optical Digital Imaging Records Management System (ODIRMS) to the Official Military Personnel File (OMPF) electronic platform. The MARADMIN consolidates Marine Corps record-keeping into the modernized OMPF system accessed through Marine Online. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines accessing personal records. Pull OMPF through MOL.
- S-1 staff and admin. Apply OMPF submission procedures.
- HQMC MMPB-22. Owns the OMPF transition.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 1070.1. Official Military Personnel File. Order receiving the transition.
- MCO P1070.12K. Marine Corps Individual Records Administration Manual (IRAM).
- MARADMIN 537/23. Command and Marine Profile application. Companion modernization.
