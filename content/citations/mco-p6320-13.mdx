## Citation clarification

There is no standalone Marine Corps Order officially numbered MCO P6320.13. Authors and inspection items have referenced this number in error. Marine Corps health care policies issue under the 6000 Medical and Dental Services series and execute jointly with the Department of the Navy through BUMED and the Defense Health Agency.

## Where the actual policy lives

- BUMEDINST 6320 series. Navy Bureau of Medicine and Surgery instructions covering clinical operations.
- MCO 6300 series. Marine Corps Medical and Dental Services policy.
- MCO 6320.4. Health Care Quality Assurance Policies for Operating Forces. The closest substantive companion order.
- Defense Health Agency (DHA) policy.
- MCRAMM (MCO 1001R.1L) Chapter 6. Reserve medical standards.
- DODI 6025.19. Individual Medical Readiness.

## How to fix authoring references

Replace any inline citation to MCO P6320.13 with the specific underlying instruction that governs the topic. For clinical quality assurance, cite MCO 6320.4. For individual medical readiness, cite DODI 6025.19. For Reserve medical standards, cite MCRAMM Chapter 6. For Navy BUMED policy, cite the relevant BUMEDINST.
