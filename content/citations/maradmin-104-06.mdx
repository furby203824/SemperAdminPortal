## Scope

MARADMIN 104/06 publishes an awards update authorizing unit awards in NAVMC 2922 and updating campaign medal eligibility. The MARADMIN follows the standard awards update structure with JMUA, MUC, and CMC Certificate of Commendation entries. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Commanders endorsing unit awards. Apply the NAVMC 2922 entries authorized in this MARADMIN.
- Adjutants and S-1 staff. Process awards through MCTFS unit diary.
- HQMC MMMA. Adjudicates eligibility.

## Connection to higher policy

- SECNAVINST 1650.1H. Navy and Marine Corps Awards Manual.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- NAVMC 2922. Marine Corps Unit Awards Manual.
- MARADMIN 211/10, MARADMIN 398/10, MARADMIN 692/20, MARADMIN 379/22. Subsequent awards updates.
