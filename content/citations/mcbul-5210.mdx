## Scope

MCBul 5210 announces upcoming changes to the Marine Corps Records Management Program Directive, MCO 5210.11F, while the parent order finishes staffing. The bulletin sets interim policy for Electronic Records Management (ERM), Capstone email capture, Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) safeguards, Zero Trust posture, government-owned device use, and the Command Historical Program. HQMC Administration and Resource Management Division (AR), Publishing and Logistics Management Branch (ARD), Records Management Program publishes the bulletin to galvanize phases one and two of the Three-Phased Approach to ERM Compliance and to prepare phase three. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Commanders and civilian leaders. Sign NAVMC 10030 within 90 days of assuming command. Appoint a CDRM in writing. Endorse the unit Records Management Program. Respond to quarterly Capstone ETMS2 taskers.
- Command Designated Records Managers (CDRMs) at E-6 or GS-9 or higher. No MOS restriction. Drive ERM migration, run training compliance, manage Capstone email capture, upload NAVMC 10030 and training rosters to CROSS.
- Site Collection Administrators. Optional appointment. Technical and instructional liaison between the CDRM and the PEO Digital Workplace Services (DWS) team.
- HQMC M&RA Manpower Management Division, Senior Leader Management Branch (MMSL). Quarterly roster feed of general officers and senior executives to ARD Records.
- HQMC AR/ARD Records. Program owner. Files monthly status reports to the Director, Marine Corps Staff. Codifies the Marine Corps Capstone list. Pushes quarterly Capstone updates to the Marine Corps Cyberspace Operations Group (MCCOG).
- Marine Corps Cyberspace Operations Group (MCCOG). Receives quarterly Capstone list updates.
- S-1 staff and adjutants. Maintain inspection evidence for IGMC FAC 5210 Records Management.
- IG inspectors. Verify CDRM appointment, NAVMC 10030 currency, CROSS posture, and training rosters during FAC 5210 visits.

## Commander action items pulled from the bulletin

1. Sign NAVMC 10030, Commander's Records Management Acknowledgement Agreement, within 90 days of assuming command. If already in command at publication, sign within 90 days of 2025-08-29. Provide the signed form to the CDRM for CROSS upload.
2. Appoint a CDRM at E-6 or GS-9 or higher in writing. The CDRM does not need MOS 0102 or 0111.
3. Confirm the command is registered in CROSS.
4. Migrate shared drive content to SharePoint Online under MARADMIN 331/24 and the Shared Drive Repository (SDR) Getting Started Guide.
5. Notify ARD Records of every Capstone official change via ETMS2 task to USMC HQMC AR ARD RECORDS. Include rank, name, billet title, NIPR email, SIPR email, and estimated rotation date.
6. Respond to the quarterly Capstone Officials Quarterly Update ETMS2 tasker.
7. Enforce annual records management training for every Marine, contractor (when contractually required), and civilian. The course is MarineNet M01RMT0700 MC Records Management Course Every Marine's Responsibility. Waypoints and CROSS host equivalent training.
8. Oversee Form NA-1005 submissions to update Capstone official data under references (e), (f), and (g).
9. Mark records with the correct CUI category under DoDI 5200.48.
10. Adopt the never trust always verify Zero Trust posture across staffing, training, and professional development.
11. Conduct command business on government-owned mobile devices. Use Microsoft Teams Chat for managed messaging. When mission needs require short message service traffic, forward the record copy to the user's official Marine Corps electronic messaging account within 20 days under 44 U.S.C. Section 2911.
12. Run the Command Historical Program. Maintain the command historical summary file and prepare command chronologies under MCO 5750.1H Chapter 5 and Appendix A, as modified by MARADMIN 200/25.

## CDRM action items pulled from the bulletin

1. Oversee ERM migration across the organization. Appoint support personnel within sections, directorates, and programs needing special attention.
2. Upload the current organizational structure to CROSS.
3. Receive the signed NAVMC 10030 and upload to the unit CROSS page under the Documents tab, Miscellaneous folder, within 90 days of bulletin release.
4. Coordinate with the unit Site Collection Administrator. Request leadership identify one if none is appointed.
5. Submit Capstone SIPR email capture updates through the quarterly ETMS2 tasker issued by ARD Records.
6. Monitor annual records management training compliance. Upload the unit training roster to CROSS.
