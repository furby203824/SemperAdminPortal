## Scope

MCRP 7-20A.2 is the Marine Corps Reference Publication on Event and Exercise Design. It is one of five companion reference publications in the MCRP 7-20A series supporting the doctrinal framework of MCTP 7-20A Unit Training Management. The publication provides guidance on designing individual and collective training events and exercises to meet training objectives tied to the Mission Essential Task List (METL) and the Training and Readiness (T&R) event standards.

## Note on Portal Citation Label

Portal content pages reference this publication as "MCRP 7-20A.2 Training Plan Development." The authoritative MCPEL title is "Event and Exercise Design." Both labels resolve to this entry. The "Training Plan Development" label more accurately describes MCRP 7-20A.1.

## MCRP 7-20A Series

The MCRP 7-20A series provides reference-level companion guides to MCTP 7-20A Unit Training Management. All five publications carry an effective date of January 2023.

- MCRP 7-20A.1 Training Plan Design. METL-to-plan translation and training plan architecture.
- MCRP 7-20A.2 Event and Exercise Design (this publication). Individual and collective event design and exercise construction.
- MCRP 7-20A.3 Simulation Training Guide. Integration of simulation resources into the training plan.
- MCRP 7-20A.4 Evaluations and Assessments. T&R event evaluation standards and assessment methods.
- MCRP 7-20A.5 Training Data Management. MCTIMS data entry, training record management, and reporting.
