## Scope

SECNAVINST 5730.5K establishes the policies and procedures for the conduct of congressional affairs in the Department of the Navy (DON). The Chief of Legislative Affairs (CLA) is the SECNAV principal staff assistant for legislative functions. The Legislative Assistant to the Commandant of the Marine Corps (LA to CMC) holds the equivalent role for Marine Corps matters. Signed by SECNAV 1 May 2018. Applicability is the DON.

## Audience

- Chief of Legislative Affairs (CLA). The DON principal legislative officer.
- Legislative Assistant to the Commandant of the Marine Corps (LA to CMC).
- Marine Corps Office of Legislative Affairs (OLA) staff.
- ASN(FM&C) Appropriations Matters Office (FMBE).
- HQMC OLA caseworkers.
- S-1 and adjutants handling constituent inquiries.
- Commanders responding to congressional correspondence.

## Key sections

- Paragraph 1 Purpose. Establish DON congressional affairs policy.
- Paragraph 2 Cancellation. Cancels SECNAVINST 5730.5J.
- Paragraph 3 Applicability. DON wide.
- Paragraph 4 Policy. CLA is SECNAV principal staff assistant for legislative functions, except appropriations vested in ASN(FM&C).
- Paragraph 5 Responsibilities. CLA, LA to CMC, ASN(FM&C), and DON component heads.
- Enclosure 1. Mission, Functions and Procedures for Legislative Matters.
