## Scope

MARADMIN 379/22 publishes an awards update for the Marine Corps. The MARADMIN authorizes unit awards in NAVMC 2922 and updates campaign medal eligibility. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Commanders endorsing unit awards. Apply the NAVMC 2922 entries authorized in this MARADMIN.
- Adjutants and S-1 staff. Process awards in MCTFS unit diary and iAPS.
- HQMC MMMA. Adjudicates eligibility.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoDI 1348.33. DoD Military Decorations and Awards Program.
- SECNAV M-1650.1. Navy and Marine Corps Awards Manual.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine
