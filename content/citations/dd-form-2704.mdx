## Scope

DD Form 2704 records the victim or witness election on post-trial notification of prisoner status changes. The form captures the victim or witness identifying information, the case data (incident number, installation, court-martial caption, accused name, last four of SSN), and the elections governing notice of confinement status changes, parole, release, transfer, and escape. Prescribed by DoDI 1325.07. Updated 19 March 2025. CUI when filled in.

## Audience

- Trial counsel and Staff Judge Advocate (SJA) offices. Issue DD 2704 to victims and witnesses post-trial.
- Confinement facilities. Receive DD 2704 to drive notification obligations during confinement.
- Marine Corps Brigs and Department of the Navy correctional facilities.
- Victim Witness Liaison. Tracks election forms.

## Key fields

- Section 1 Administrative Information. Incident number, installation, city, state, zip, court-martial caption, accused name, last four of SSN.
- Section 2 Victim or Witness Identification. Name, current address, telephone, email.
- Section 3 Election. Notification of changes in prisoner status. Yes or no election. Specific events covered. Parole, mandatory supervised release, transfer to another facility, escape, work release, furlough, expiration of sentence, release.
- Section 4 Certification. Signature blocks for victim or witness and the trial counsel.

## Privacy Act

42 USC 10607 et seq. 18 USC 1512 et seq. DoDI 1325.07. DoDI 1030.2. DoDD 1030.1. CUI when filled in. Disclosure is voluntary, but failure to provide identifying information prevents notification.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- DoDI 1325.07. Prescribing authority.
- DoDI 1030.2 and DoDD 1030.1. Victim and Witness Assistance.
- MCO 5800.16 Vol. 9. Marine Corps Victim Witness Assistance Program (VWAP).
- DD Form 2701. Companion form delivered at the start of the case.

## Status

Active. Issued post-trial for any case with covered victims or witnesses.
