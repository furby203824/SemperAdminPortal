## Scope

MARADMIN 691/21 announces the Calendar Year 2022 Continuation Pay Program for Blended Retirement System (BRS) participants. The MARADMIN sets eligibility, multipliers, and processing rules for Continuation Pay paid at 12 years of service. Applicability is BRS-enrolled Marines.

## Audience

- BRS-enrolled Marines approaching 12 years of service. Apply for Continuation Pay.
- Disbursing officers. Process Continuation Pay under DoD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 1.
- Career planners. Track Continuation Pay eligibility.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 1. Continuation Pay framework.
- NDAA FY2016. BRS authorizing legislation.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 1741.13. Blended Retirement System.
