## Originals Versus Photocopies

Many life event packages require certified originals.

- Marriage certificates. Certified original from the issuing county.
- Birth certificates. Certified original.
- Death certificates. Certified original.
- Court orders. Certified original.
- Divorce decrees. Certified, final, not the petition.

Photocopies fail at the first PAC review. The Marine goes home, returns to county for the certified copy, and the 10-day window slips.

<Callout variant="warning" title="Certified means embossed seal or color stamp">
A photocopy of a certified document does not count as certified. The receiving office checks for raised seal, color stamp, or county notary signature. If the document does not have those marks, the document is a photocopy.
</Callout>

## Signatures Before You Route

Most packages carry multiple signature blocks.

- Member.
- Spouse where applicable.
- Witness.
- Section leader.
- Endorsing official.

Verify every block is signed before the package routes. A missing signature returns the package.

## Section Master Checklist

Build a section-level master checklist covering every package type your Marines run.

- Type.
- Required documents.
- Required signatures.
- Time window.
- S-1 or PAC point of contact.

Refresh annually or whenever a MARADMIN changes a process.
