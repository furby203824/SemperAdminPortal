## Scope

MARADMIN 613/25 provided advance notification of changes to the Marine Corps Physical Fitness Test and Body Composition Evaluation (BCE) process. The MARADMIN set the framework for the waist-to-height ratio (WHtR) methodology effective 2026-01-01 and the transition away from height and weight as a body composition measure. The framework was amended by Change 1 published in MARADMIN 066/26. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines undergoing semiannual BCE. Apply the WHtR methodology.
- Body composition evaluators. Apply same-sex evaluator rule and self-tensioning taping device.
- Commanders. Confirm Marines are evaluated under the revised process.

## Connection to higher policy

- SECWAR Memorandum dated 2025-12-18. Additional guidance on military fitness standards.
