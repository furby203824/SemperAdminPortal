## Scope

Volume 7A of DOD Financial Management Regulation 7000.14-R sets military pay policy for active duty and reserve component service members. The Office of the Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller) publishes the FMR. Each chapter updates on its own cadence and carries its own effective date in the header.

## Audience

Every Marine touches Vol 7A indirectly through pay. Disbursing clerks read it directly. S-1 and IPAC reference it for entitlement decisions. Commanders rely on it for authority to approve special pays and advance pay.

## Coverage by chapter family

- Chapters 1 through 4. Basic pay and definitions.
- Chapters 5 through 18. Special and incentive pays.
- Chapters 25 through 32. Allowances, advance pay, and travel allowances.
- Chapters 33 through 45. Deductions, garnishments, separation payments.
- Chapters 50 and above. Special-population pay rules including Reserve mobilization.
