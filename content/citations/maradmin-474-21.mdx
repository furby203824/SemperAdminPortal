## Scope

MARADMIN 474/21 publishes updated Enlisted Professional Military Education (PME) requirements by grade. The MARADMIN sets the resident and non-resident course completion requirements for each enlisted grade. Subsequent updates appear in MARADMIN 630/24 and MARADMIN 631/24 for Active Duty and Selected Marine Corps Reserve respectively. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines and career planners. Apply EPME requirements by grade for promotion eligibility.
- HQMC TECOM. Owns the requirement framework.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 1553.4B. Professional Military Education (PME).
- MARADMIN 630/24. Updated EPME Requirements for Active Duty and Active Reserve Marines. Current Active Component framework.
- MARADMIN 631/24. Updated EPME Requirements for Selected Marine Corps Reserve. Current SMCR framework.
- MARADMIN 222/26. Sergeant TIS and Staff Sergeant Promotion Process. Cross-reference for Sergeant PME sequencing.

## Status

Released 2021-09. Originator CG TECOM Quantico VA. Superseded by MARADMIN 630/24 and MARADMIN 631/24.
