## Scope

MARADMIN 145/24 solicits qualified Marines for lateral move (LATMOV) into the Marine Corps Community Services (4133) PMOS. The 4133 PMOS supports Marine and Family Programs, MCCS resale and recreation operations, and family readiness initiatives. The MARADMIN runs under the broader FY24 retention campaign framework. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Active component Marines considering a lateral move into 4133. Eligible to apply.
- Career planners. Process LATMOV applications.
- HQMC MMEA-1. Adjudicates LATMOV requests.

## LATMOV process

- Marines submit through Marine Online (MOL) under the Career Planner module.
- Applications include command endorsement.
- HQMC MMEA-1 reviews against open boatspaces.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- NAVMC 1200.1K. Military Occupational Specialties Manual. 4133 PMOS requirements.
- MARADMIN 014/25. FY25 Active Component Enlisted Marine Lateral Move Opportunities. Companion LATMOV announcement.
- MCO 1040.31. Enlisted Retention and Career Development Program.

Released 2024-03. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MM. Applicability is the Active Component. POCs MMEA-1, 703-432-9120. Pull current LATMOV availability through the unit Career Counselor.
