## Scope

MCRP 7-20A.1 is the Marine Corps Reference Publication on Training Plan Design. It provides guidance on translating METL tasks into a coherent training plan, integrating T&R event requirements, mission analysis, and available resources into the unit training calendar. Part of the MCRP 7-20A companion series to MCTP 7-20A Unit Training Management.

## Note on Portal Citation Label

Portal content pages reference this publication as "MCRP 7-20A.1 Mission Essential Task Development." The authoritative MCPEL title is "Training Plan Design." Both labels resolve to this entry. METL task development is addressed in this publication's training plan architecture content.
