## Scope

MARADMIN 185/25 cancels obsolete promotion MARADMINs and announces upcoming changes to MCO P1400.32D, Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions. The MARADMIN clears stale references in preparation for the next revision of the order. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Career planners and S-1 staff. Discontinue reference to cancelled promotion MARADMINs.
- HQMC MMPB-11. Owns the upcoming MCO P1400.32D revision.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P1400.32D. Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions. Order receiving the upcoming revision.
- MARADMIN 222/26. Forthcoming Changes to TIS for Promotion to Sergeant. Companion update.
