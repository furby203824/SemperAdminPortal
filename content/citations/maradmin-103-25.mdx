## Scope

MARADMIN 103/25 announces noncompetitive Active Duty Corporal to Sergeant promotion procedure for Recruiter SDA. The MARADMIN sets the criteria and promotion warrant process for Corporals assigned to Recruiter SDA who meet the eligibility standard. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Corporals on Recruiter SDA. Eligible for noncompetitive promotion to Sergeant.
- Career planners and IPACs. Process the promotion warrant.
- HQMC MMPB-11. Adjudicates eligibility.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P1400.32D. Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions.
- MARADMIN 181/26. FY28 SDA Campaign. References this MARADMIN.
