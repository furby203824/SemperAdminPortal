## Scope

MCO P1400.32D, Volume 2 of the Marine Corps Promotion Manual, sets policy and procedures for enlisted promotions. The manual covers cutting scores, composite scores, time-in-grade and time-in-service requirements, PME completion for promotion, special-promotion programs, meritorious promotion, frocking, and Recruiter Specialty Promotion. Short title MARCORPROMMAN Vol 2, ENLPROM.

## Audience

Every enlisted Marine reads this manual to understand their promotion path. Career planners and monitors apply it to assignment and promotion decisions. S-1 and unit administrators process the promotion warrants and entries in MCTFS. Commanders sign warrants and frocking authorizations under this manual.

## Change history

- CH 1. 17 December 2008. Revised pages across Chapters 1 through 5 plus the Index. Added pages 1-10A, 1-12A, 1-16A, 2-16A, and 4-6A. Adjusted composite-score and cutting-score policy.
- CH 2. 14 June 2012. Substantial revision. Important changes include PME requirements for promotion, combat-zone policy, and updates spanning Chapters 1 through 6.

## Manual structure

- Chapter 1. Eligibility, selection, and authority for enlisted promotions.
- Chapter 2. Composite scores, cutting scores, and the promotion zone.
- Chapter 3. Special-promotion programs and meritorious promotion.
- Chapter 4. Senior staff non-commissioned officer (SNCO) and staff sergeant promotions.
- Chapter 5. Reserve promotions.
- Chapter 6. Administrative procedures.

## Authority

Commandant of the Marine Corps publishes the manual. Manpower Management Promotion Branch (MMPR-2) maintains it.

## Status

Active. Both CH 1 and CH 2 are in force. The basic order plus the two changes form the operative document. Pair with MCO 1610.7B for the underlying performance evaluation framework.
