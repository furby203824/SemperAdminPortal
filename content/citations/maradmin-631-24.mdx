## Scope

MARADMIN 631/24 publishes updated EPME requirements for the Selected Marine Corps Reserve (SMCR). Companion message to MARADMIN 630/24 for the Active Component. Applicability is the SMCR.

## Audience

- SMCR enlisted Marines. Apply EPME requirements by grade.
- SMCR career planners. Track EPME completion.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 1553.4B. Professional Military Education (PME).
- MARADMIN 630/24. Active Duty and Active Reserve EPME companion.
- MARADMIN 474/21. Earlier EPME Requirements by Grade.

## Status

Released 2024-12. Originator CG TECOM Quantico VA. Applicability is the SMCR.
