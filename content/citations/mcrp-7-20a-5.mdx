## Scope

MCRP 7-20A.5 is the Marine Corps Reference Publication on Training Data Management. It provides guidance on MCTIMS data entry standards, training record management, and training readiness reporting. Chapter 2 defines UTM roles and functions within the unit training management structure. Part of the MCRP 7-20A companion series to MCTP 7-20A Unit Training Management.

## Note on Portal Citation Label

Portal content pages reference this publication as "MCRP 7-20A.5 Unit Training Management Roles and Functions." The authoritative MCPEL title is "Training Data Management." Both labels resolve to this entry. UTM roles and functions appear in Chapter 2 of this publication.
