## Scope

DODI 1341.13 establishes policy and assigns responsibilities for the transfer of unused Post-9/11 GI Bill education benefits from servicemembers to eligible family members. The instruction governs eligibility criteria, transfer rules, additional service obligation, revocation, and administration through the Department of Veterans Affairs. Applicability is the uniformed services and dependents of servicemembers electing transfer.

## Audience

- Servicemembers eligible for the Post-9/11 GI Bill considering Transfer of Education Benefits (TEB).
- Spouses and children of servicemembers electing transfer.
- Career counselors and education service officers.
- S-1 admin specialists processing TEB requests.
- Department of Veterans Affairs Education Service.

## Common confusion

Authors and inspection items occasionally cite "DODI 1341.13 SGLI Program". That tagging is wrong. SGLI SOES policy lives in DODI 1341.14, not 1341.13. DODI 1341.13 covers the Post-9/11 GI Bill Transfer of Education Benefits exclusively.
