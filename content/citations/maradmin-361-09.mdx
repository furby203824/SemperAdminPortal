## Scope

MARADMIN 361/09 set Hazardous Duty Incentive Pay (HDIP) authority and rates for Marines performing demolition duty. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- EOD Technicians and personnel performing demolition duty. Eligible for HDIP.
- Disbursing officers. Process HDIP under DoD FMR Volume 7A.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD FMR Volume 7A. HDIP rate framework.
- 37 U.S.C. Section 301. Special pay authority.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 728/12. Hazardous Duty Incentive Pay Demolition Duty. Successor guidance.

## Status

Released 2009-06. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MP. Superseded by MARADMIN 728/12 for current HDIP demolition framework.
