## Scope

MCO 6320.4 (joint with OPNAVINST 6320.7A) establishes policy, prescribes procedures, and assigns responsibilities for the quality assurance of health care provided to the operating forces. The Secretary of the Navy holds policy oversight of the Clinical Quality Management Program within the Department of the Navy. BUMED-M3B62 is the program office of record. The order references DOD 6025.13-R, BUMEDINST 6010.13, BUMEDINST 6320.66E, and OPNAVINST 6400.1C.
