## Scope

The DTMO Per Diem Rate Lookup is the authoritative DoD tool for current per diem rates for CONUS, OCONUS, foreign locations, and US territories. The lookup serves as the single point of reference for lodging, meals and incidental expenses (M&IE), and total per diem rates required for Joint Travel Regulations (JTR) compliance. Per diem rates update monthly for CONUS (effective the first of each month) and quarterly for OCONUS and foreign locations.

## Audience

- Travelers preparing Defense Travel System (DTS) authorizations and vouchers.
- Travel approving officials and authorizing officials.
- IPAC travel clerks and S-1 pay clerks.
- DTS Non-DTS Entry Agents (NDEA).
- Commanders authorizing TDY travel.
- Personnel processing PCS travel claims.

## What the lookup provides

- Lodging rate. Maximum reimbursable nightly lodging.
- Meals and incidental expenses (M&IE) rate. Daily meals and incidentals.
- First and last day of travel rate (M&IE x 75 percent).
- Total per diem rate (lodging plus M&IE).
- Standard CONUS rate. Default for CONUS locations not listed individually.
- OCONUS rates issued by the State Department for foreign areas.
- DoD OCONUS rates for non-foreign OCONUS locations including Alaska, Hawaii, and US territories.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- DoD 7000.14-R Volume 9. Travel Policy.
- Joint Travel Regulations (JTR). Per diem application rules.
- 5 USC 5701-5739. Statutory travel and per diem authority.
- 41 CFR Chapters 300-304. Federal Travel Regulation (FTR).
- DoDI 5154.31 Volume 5. Per Diem, Travel and Transportation Allowance Committee.
- DOS Standardized Regulations. Foreign per diem rate methodology.

## Status

Active. CONUS rates updated monthly. OCONUS and foreign rates updated quarterly. Live interactive tool, no static publication number.
