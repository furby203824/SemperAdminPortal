## Scope

MARADMIN 092/21 updates MARADMIN 414/20, FY21 Hardship Duty Pay - Tempo (HDP-T) Program Details. The MARADMIN refines eligibility and processing rules. Applicability is active and reserve component Marines.

## Audience

- Marines eligible for HDP-T. Confirm rate and eligibility.
- Disbursing officers. Process HDP-T payments.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 510/14. Authorization for Hardship Duty Pay - Tempo (HDP-T).
- MARADMIN 619/14. HDP-T Program Details.
- MARADMIN 414/20. FY21 HDP-T Program Details. Updated by this MARADMIN.
