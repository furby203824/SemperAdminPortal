## Scope

MARADMIN 037/23 announced the FY23 USMC Chief Warrant Officer Five through Chief Warrant Officer Three Promotion Selection Board results. The MARADMIN listed selectees by name, EDIPI, and PMOS for promotion to CWO3, CWO4, and CWO5. Applicability is the Active Component CWO population.

## Audience

- Selected CWOs. Apply the promotion sequence under the Promotion Manual.
- Career planners. Process promotions through MCTFS.
- HQMC MMPB-10. Owns the lineal staff and promotion processing.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P1400.31D. Officer Promotion Manual.
- MARADMIN 628/23. FY26 Officer Promotion Selection Boards. Companion schedule.

## Status

Released 2023-01. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MM. Applicability is the Active Component.
