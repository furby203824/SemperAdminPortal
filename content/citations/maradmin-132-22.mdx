## Scope

MARADMIN 132/22 provides Department of the Navy Personal Military Decoration (PMD) guidance for the 2022 Permanent Change of Station (PCS) season. Award originators identify highly performing Marines deserving of DoN PMDs and submit via the Improved Awards Processing System (iAPS). Nominations must reach the final awarding authority at least 60 days prior to the planned presentation date. Awards approved by CMC reach CMC (MMPB-31) no later than 60 days prior. SECNAV-approved awards reach CMC (MMPB-31) no later than 90 days prior.

## Audience

- Unit Award Administrators (UAAs). Download the outbound roster from the Marine Online Outbound Interview Module. Publish to command leadership.
- Award originators. Identify nominees. Submit through iAPS.
- Awarding authorities. Adjudicate per the delegation framework.
- Retiring and separating Marines. Receive PMDs prior to detachment or terminal leave. The meritorious period end date should be two weeks before terminal leave to allow MCTFS recording on the DD-214.

## Award processing rules

- Summary of Action (SOA). Two-page limit per SECNAV M-1650.1. Verify length using the iAPS "print PDF" button.
- Citation character limits. Meritorious Service Medal and above 1670 characters in standard sentence case. Navy and Marine Corps Commendation Medal and below 1250 characters in all caps.
- Citation pitch. 8 to 12 point. Margins 0.5 inch to 1.0 inch.
- Certificate and citation upload to the iAPS "certificate" section. Do not attach letters of transmittal or the NAVMC 11533.
- Meritorious Mast. Recommended the approver originate the award and upload the signed certificate before approval.

## Expedite requests

CMC (MMPB-31) takes action on expedite requests in cases of retirement, relief and appointments, or change of command. Senior endorsing General Officer or Chief of Staff signs the request with explanation. Expedite reduces the votes required by the HQMC Military Awards Board but does not guarantee completion by the desired date.
