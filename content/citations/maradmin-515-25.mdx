## Scope

MARADMIN 515/25 announces non-competitive Intended Military Occupational Specialty (IMOS) promotions for the Active Component. Builds on MARADMIN 469/24. Applies to Marines in training for a new PMOS. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Marines in PMOS training. Eligible for non-competitive IMOS promotion.
- Career planners and IPACs. Process the promotion warrant.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P1400.32D. Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions.
- MARADMIN 469/24. FY25 IMOS Promotions. Predecessor framework.

## Status

Released 2025-10. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MM. Applicability is the Active Component.
