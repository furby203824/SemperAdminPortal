## Scope

MARADMIN 007/25 cancels MARADMIN 178/24, Reserve Component Telecommute Policy Update. The cancellation issues under the DoD Memorandum for Senior Pentagon Leadership, Commanders of the Combatant Commands, Defense Agency and DoD Field Activity Directors dated 2025-01-31.

## Audience

- Reserve component leaders and unit administrators. Discontinue reference to MARADMIN 178/24.
- Reserve Affairs Policy Branch (RAP-1). Adjudicates questions.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD Memorandum dated 2025-01-31. Drives the cancellation.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 178/24. Reserve Component Telecommute Policy Update. Cancelled by this MARADMIN.

## Status

Released 2025-02-11, DTG ``R 101847Z FEB 25``. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA RA. Release authorized by Major General Karl D. Pierson, Director, Reserve Affairs Division. POC RAP-1, 703-784-9615, rap@usmc.mil.
