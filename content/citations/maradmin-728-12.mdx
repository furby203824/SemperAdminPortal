## Scope

MARADMIN 728/12 updates Hazardous Duty Incentive Pay (HDIP) for demolition duty. The MARADMIN supersedes MARADMIN 361/09 with updated rates and eligibility. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- EOD Technicians and personnel performing demolition duty. Eligible for HDIP.
- Disbursing officers. Process HDIP under the updated rate framework.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD FMR Volume 7A. HDIP rate framework.
- 37 U.S.C. Section 301. Special pay authority.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 361/09. Prior HDIP Demolition Duty MARADMIN. Superseded by this MARADMIN.

## Status

Released 2012-12. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MP. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.
