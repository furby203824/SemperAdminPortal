## Scope

DODI 1341.14 establishes policy, assigns responsibilities, and provides direction for the use and oversight of the Servicemembers Group Life Insurance (SGLI) On-Line Enrollment System (SOES). SOES is the authoritative system of record for members with full-time SGLI coverage and the primary means for making changes to SGLI coverage and beneficiary designations. Signed 19 January 2017 by Peter Levine, performing the duties of the Under Secretary of Defense for Personnel and Readiness. Cleared for public release.

## Policy

SOES is the authoritative system of record for members with full-time SGLI coverage. SOES is the primary means for making changes to SGLI coverage and beneficiary designations. SGV 8286 forms are limited use under SOES policy.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- 38 USC Chapter 19. Servicemembers Group Life Insurance statutory authority.
- VA SGLI policy and the Office of Servicemembers Group Life Insurance (OSGLI).
- MCO P1070.12 IRAM. Individual record administration including SGLI elections.
- DD Form 93. Record of Emergency Data, related but separate from SGLI elections.

## Common confusion

Authors and inspection items occasionally cite "DODI 1341.13 SGLI Program". That citation is wrong. DODI 1341.13 governs the Post-9/11 GI Bill Transfer of Education Benefits, not SGLI. SGLI SOES policy lives in this DODI, 1341.14.
