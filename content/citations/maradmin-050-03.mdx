## Scope

MARADMIN 050/03 set early Marine Corps policy on Basic Allowance for Subsistence (BAS) deductions and per diem entitlements for Marines participating in joint force contingency operations. The MARADMIN aligned BAS handling with joint travel regulations during contingency deployment. Use this entry for historical reference. Current BAS and per diem rules sit in the JTR and DoD FMR Volume 7A.

## Audience

- Disbursing officers and Marines on contingency deployment. Apply current JTR and DoD FMR Volume 7A.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- Joint Travel Regulations. Parent travel authority.
- DoD FMR Volume 7A. Pay and allowance reference.

## Status

Released 2003-02. Originator CMC Washington DC. Historical reference. Verify against current JTR and DoD FMR Volume 7A before applying.
