## Scope

MARADMIN 457/16 expanded Active Duty Career Retention and Permanent Limited Duty (PLD) policy for wounded Marines. The MARADMIN extended PLD eligibility beyond combat wounded to include other Marines unable to fully recover but willing and able to serve in a limited capacity. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines with permanent medical limitations interested in remaining on active duty. Apply for EPLD status.
- Medical Evaluation Boards. Adjudicate fitness for continued service.
- Wounded Warrior Regiment. Coordinates case management.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 228/06. Active Duty Career Retention and Permanent Limited Duty (PLD) Policy for Combat Wounded. Original framework.
- SECNAVINST 1850.4. DON Disability Evaluation Manual.
