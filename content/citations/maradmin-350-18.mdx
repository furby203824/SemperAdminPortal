## Scope

MARADMIN 350/18 establishes the Marine Corps SkillBridge Employment Training Program. The program allows transitioning Service Members to receive private-sector training, education, and apprenticeships during the last 180 days of military service. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Marines within 180 days of EAS. Eligible for SkillBridge.
- Career planners. Process SkillBridge applications.
- HQMC M&RA Marine and Family Programs. Owns the program.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoDI 1322.29. Job Training, Employment Skills Training, Apprenticeships, and Internships (JTEST-AI).

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 1700.31A. Marine Corps Transition Readiness Program (TRP). Companion framework.
- MARADMIN 573/24. TCOP Initiatives. Companion family support.

## Status

Released 2018-06. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA. Active. Verify against current SkillBridge guidance at https://skillbridge.osd.mil before applying.
