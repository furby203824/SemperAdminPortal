## Scope

MARADMIN 537/23 announces the reactivation of the Command and Marine Profile application within Marine Online (MOL). The application provides leaders with consolidated Marine readiness, performance, and personnel data. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Commanders and unit leaders. Access the Command and Marine Profile application through MOL.
- S-1 staff. Reference the application for Marine readiness data.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- Marine Corps Total Force System (MCTFS). System of record feeding the application.
- Marine Online (MOL). Hosting platform.

## Status

Released 2023-10. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.
