## Scope

MARADMIN 637/22 announces extension of the Inherent Resolve Campaign Medal (IRCM) for service in Iraq and authorization for additional eligibility periods. The MARADMIN expands the campaign phase windows. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines who served in Iraq during the qualifying periods. Eligible for IRCM.
- Commanding officers. Issue the medal under MCTFS unit diary.
- HQMC MMMA. Adjudicates eligibility.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoDI 1348.33. DoD Military Decorations and Awards Program.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- NAVMC 2922. Marine Corps Unit Awards Manual.
- MARADMIN 692/20. Awards Update including IRCM eligibility for MSG Baghdad and Erbil. Predecessor IRCM expansion.
