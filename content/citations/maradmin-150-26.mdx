## Scope

MARADMIN 150/26 announces a change to the Family Separation Allowance (FSA) rate. The MARADMIN aligns FSA payment with current DoD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 27 rates. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines receiving FSA. Apply the updated rate.
- Disbursing officers. Process FSA payments at the new rate.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 27. FSA authority and rate framework.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 573/24. TCOP Program Initiatives. Companion family support framework.

## Status

Released 2026-04. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MP. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.
