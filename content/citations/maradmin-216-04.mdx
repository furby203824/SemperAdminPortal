## Scope

MARADMIN 216/04 set early Marine Corps policy for BAH waivers for Marines attending Professional Military Education (PME). The MARADMIN was cancelled by MCO 7220.56A dated 2015-08-31, which provides the current PCS for PME or Training BAH waiver framework (Chapter 3 of the order).

## Audience

- Marines attending PME. Apply the current MCO 7220.56A Chapter 3 framework.
- HQMC MMIB-3. Current waiver authority.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 7220.56A. BAH at locations other than PDS. Chapter 3 covers PCS for PME or Training under 12 months and over 12 months. Cancels this MARADMIN.

## Status

Released 2004. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA. Cancelled by MCO 7220.56A. Use this entry for historical context only.
