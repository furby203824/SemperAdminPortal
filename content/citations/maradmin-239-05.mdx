## Scope

MARADMIN 239/05 set early Marine Corps policy for Basic Allowance for Housing (BAH) at locations other than the Permanent Duty Station (PDS). The MARADMIN was cancelled by MCO 7220.56A dated 2015-08-31, which provides the current BAH waiver framework.

## Audience

- Marines and S-1 staff. Apply the current BAH waiver framework in MCO 7220.56A.
- HQMC MMIB-3. Current waiver authority.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 7220.56A. Current BAH at locations other than PDS policy. Cancels this MARADMIN.

## Status

Released 2005. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA. Cancelled by MCO 7220.56A. Use this entry for historical context only.
