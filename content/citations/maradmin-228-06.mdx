## Scope

MARADMIN 228/06 announced the Marine Corps Active Duty Career Retention and Permanent Limited Duty (PLD) Policy for combat wounded Marines. The MARADMIN allows combat wounded Marines to remain on active duty in a permanent limited duty status under specified medical and operational criteria.

## Audience

- Combat wounded Marines. Eligible to request PLD status to remain on active duty.
- Wounded Warrior Regiment. Coordinates PLD applications and case management.
- Medical Evaluation Boards (MEBs). Adjudicate medical fitness for continued service.
- HQMC Manpower Management Separations and Retirements Branch. Adjudicates PLD applications.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- SECNAVINST 1850.4. DON Disability Evaluation Manual.
- MCO 1740.18. Wounded Warrior Regiment Order.

## Status

Released 2006-05. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA. Applicability is combat wounded Marines on active duty. Active.
