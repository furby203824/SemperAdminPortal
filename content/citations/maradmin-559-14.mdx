## Scope

MARADMIN 559/14 publishes guidance for archiving Service Treatment Records (STRs) for all United States Marines. The MARADMIN sets the STR retention, archive, and access framework supporting Department of Veterans Affairs claims and longitudinal medical records. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marine Corps medical units. Apply STR archive procedures.
- Departing Marines. Verify STR completeness before separation.
- Department of Veterans Affairs. Receives archived STRs for veteran claims.

## Connection to higher policy

- BUMEDINST 6150.43. Naval Health Records.
- 38 U.S.C. VA records access framework.
