## Scope

MARADMIN 028/24 cancels MARADMIN 602/24, Guiding Principles for the Ethical Use of Artificial Intelligence by Communication Strategy and Operations. Replaced concurrently by MARADMIN 635/24.

## Audience

- COMMSTRAT community. Apply the current MARADMIN 635/24 framework.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 602/24. Cancelled by this MARADMIN.
- MARADMIN 635/24. Current Ethical AI framework for COMMSTRAT.

## Status

Released 2024-12. Originator CMC CD Washington DC. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.
