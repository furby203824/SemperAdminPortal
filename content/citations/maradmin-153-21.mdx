## Scope

MARADMIN 153/21 clarifies the promotion recommendation process and remedial promotions framework for junior enlisted Marines. Superseded by MARADMIN 559/23 Automation framework. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Commanding officers. Apply current MARADMIN 559/23 remedial promotion framework.
- Career planners and S-1 staff. Track NOT RECOMMENDED entries.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P1400.32D. Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions.
- MARADMIN 559/23. Junior Enlisted Remedial Promotions Automation. Current framework.

Released 2021-03. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MM. Superseded by MARADMIN 559/23.
