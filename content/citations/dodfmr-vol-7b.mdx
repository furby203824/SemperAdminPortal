## Scope

DoD Financial Management Regulation Volume 7B sets military pay policy for retired pay. The Office of the Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller) publishes the FMR. Volume 7B covers retired pay computation, entitlement, deductions, election of options, Survivor Benefit Plan (SBP), Reserve retirement and the Reserve Retired Pay system, Combat-Related Special Compensation (CRSC), Concurrent Retirement and Disability Pay (CRDP), and the cost-of-living adjustment process. Each chapter updates on its own cadence and carries its own effective date.

## Audience

- Marine Corps Manpower Management Separation and Retirement (MMSR).
- Retired Marines and Fleet Marine Corps Reserve (FMCR) members.
- Defense Finance and Accounting Service (DFAS) Retired Pay.
- S-1 and IPAC retirement processing staff.
- Career planners advising Marines on retirement option elections.
- SBP counselors.

## Coverage by chapter family

- Chapters 1 through 13. Retired pay entitlement and computation.
- Chapters 14 through 35. Retired pay deductions and the Survivor Benefit Plan.
- Chapters 50 through 67. Reserve retirement, points accounting, and Reserve Retired Pay.
- Chapters 70 and above. CRSC, CRDP, and special compensation rules.
