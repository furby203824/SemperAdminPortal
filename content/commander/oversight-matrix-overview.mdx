## Authority

MCO 5040.6K, Enclosure 1, Chapter 2 establishes the Critical or Required Evaluation (CoRE) and CoRE Plus Functional Areas every commander is accountable for under the IGMC inspection program. This matrix consolidates the personal commander actions across the 33 functional areas where you carry a signature, an appointment, or a sign-off requirement.

## What you sign

Every row below is anchored to a specific Marine Corps Order. The "Action" column summarizes the personal commander action surfaced by the source MCO. Click the FA number to open the citation. Verified rows carry the citation's `lastVerified` date as authoritative. Unverified rows carry a marker; the action statement is awaiting a source-text walk against the originating MCO before being treated as authoritative.

## Exception path

Where unit mission, structure, or assignment removes a Functional Area from your scope, document the covering relationship in your CGIP directive. Waivers route to IGMC using the Appendix E template in MCO 5040.6K.

## The matrix

The matrix below is summarized from the IGMC FACs. Treat unverified rows as a starting point for your CGIP walk, not as the final word.

| Functional Area | Order | Required commander action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Readiness and reports | [MCO 3000.13](/citations/mco-3000-13b) | Assess and sign monthly C-levels in DRRS-MC. Appoint Readiness Officer or SNCO in writing. |
| Training (UTM) | [MCO 1553.3](/citations/mco-1553-3c) | Publish unit METL in MCTIMS within 45 days of command. Publish annual Training Guidance. |
| Force Preservation | [MCO 1500.60](/citations/mco-1500-60) | Codify FPC process in written SOP. Chair monthly councils. Retain CIRRAS appointment letters. |
| Career Planning | [MCO 1040](/citations/mco-1040-31) | Conduct EAS and RECC interviews. Review and sign all reenlistment approvals. |
| PES and FitReps | [MCO 1610](/citations/mco-1610-7) | Ensure annual PES education for all hands. Review CTR to eliminate late reports inside 30 days. |
| Request Mast | [MCO 1700.23](/citations/mco-1700-23) | Process NAVMC 11296 personally. Ensure policy familiarity via Read Boards or training rosters. |
| SAPR | [MCO 1752.5](/citations/mco-1752-5c) | Appoint SAPR Officer and VAs in writing. Meet monthly with SARC to review program status. |
| Safety (Ground) | [MCO 5100.29](/citations/mco-5100-29c) | Sign annual Command Safety Policy Statement. Chair Command Safety Council quarterly. |
| Aviation Safety | [MCO 3750.6](/citations/mco-3750-6) | Chair Enlisted and Aviation Safety Councils. Appoint ASO, GSO, and ASEA in writing. |
| Aviation Operations | [MCO 3710.7](/citations/mco-3710-7) | Sign or delegate flight schedule authority. Certify aircrew qualifications in writing. |
| Supply | [MCO 4400.201](/citations/mco-4400-201) | Appoint Supply Officer and Responsible Officers. Certify annual wall-to-wall inventory. |
| Conduct (PAC) | [MCO 5354.1](/citations/mco-5354-1) | Appoint EOA and EOC in writing. Ensure 85 percent completion of annual PAC training in MCTIMS. |
| Substance Abuse | [MCO 5300.17](/citations/mco-5300-17) | Appoint SACO and ASPC in writing. Oversee monthly random urinalysis and breathalyzer testing. |
| Transitions (TRP) | [MCO 1700.31](/citations/mco-1700-31) | Appoint UTCs in writing. Conduct commander verification on DD 2648. |
| Family Readiness | [MCO 1754.9](/citations/mco-1754-9) | Appoint DRC and URC in writing. Sign unit Family Readiness Command Philosophy annually. |
| Physical Security | [MCO 5530](/citations/mco-5530-14a) | Appoint Physical Security Officer. Approve and annually review the organization-specific Physical Security Plan. |
| Cyber Security | [MCO 5239](/citations/mco-5239-2b) | Publish signed policy governing use of personal portable electronic devices in government workspaces. |
| Records Management | [MCO 5210](/citations/mco-5210-11) | Sign NAVMC 10030 within 90 days of command. Appoint CDRM in CROSS. |
| Intel Oversight | [MCO 3800.2](/citations/mco-3800-2) | Certify annual IO training. Report questionable activities to IGMC within one business day. |
| Casualty Affairs | [MCO 3040](/citations/mco-3040-4) | Appoint CACR and CACOs in writing. Ensure PNOK notification within 8 hours of incident awareness. |
| Suicide Prevention | [MCO 1720.1](/citations/mco-1720-1) | Sign program policy incorporating SMITR functions. Appoint uniformed SPPC in writing. |
| Health Services | [MCO 6000](/citations/mco-6000) | Oversee 90 percent medical readiness. Establish CSIB policy if unit maintains controlled substances. |
| Leave and Liberty | [MCO 1050](/citations/mco-1050-16a) | Publish written command directive for leave, liberty, and PTAD. |
| Postal Affairs | [MCO 5110.2](/citations/mco-5110-2) | Publish Unit Mail Handling Order. Appoint Mail Officer and at least two Clerks in writing. |
| OPSEC | [MCO 3070](/citations/mco-3070-2a) | Appoint OPSEC Manager in writing. Approve and sign the Critical Information and Indicator List. |
| Antiterrorism | [MCO 3302](/citations/mco-3302) | Appoint Level II AT Officer. Approve unit AT Plan and Random AT Measures schedule. |
| Fitness (BCP) | [MCO 6110](/citations/mco-6110-3a) | Appoint FFI and CPTR. Ensure medical evaluations are done prior to BCP and MAP assignment. |
| Housing (UH) | [MCO 11000.1](/citations/mco-11000-1) | Establish UH management policies. Approve BAH Without Dependents and CNAs. |
| Motor Transport | [MCO 11240](/citations/mco-11240) | Act as Licensing Authority for tactical vehicles. Ensure Equipment Officer appoints dispatchers in writing. |
| Voter Assist | [MCO 1742.1](/citations/mco-1742-1) | Appoint UVAO in writing. Publish written command voting assistance policy and SOP. |
| Inspections | [MCO 5040.6K](/citations/mco-5040-6k) | Stand the CGIP. Conduct subordinate-unit inspections on a 36-month cycle. Sign Corrective Action Reports for findings from IGMC and CIG inspections. |
| History | [MCO 5750](/citations/mco-5750-1h) | Assign Staff Historian by official letter. Maintain Command Historical Summary File. |
| Religious Ministries | [MCO 1730](/citations/mco-1730-6) | Ensure RMT has direct access to you. Provide private, confidential workspace for the chaplain. |

## Source verification posture

The action statements above are summarized from the IGMC FACs and the parent MCOs. They are useful as a one-glance commander view. Treat each statement as a starting point and confirm specifics against the linked citation and the IGMC FAC at `/inspections/igmc` before any signature, appointment, or policy action.

For the inspections lifecycle itself, see [Inspections lifecycle overview](/commander/inspections-lifecycle/overview).
