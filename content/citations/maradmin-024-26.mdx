## Scope

MARADMIN 024/26 announces the Calendar Year 2026 USMC Officer Non-Statutory Selection Board and Panel schedule. The MARADMIN sets convening dates for officer slating panels including Service Academy assignments, CMC Fellowships, fellowship programs, and other non-statutory boards. Individual MARADMINs publish solicitation criteria for each board.

## Audience

- Marines eligible for CY26 officer non-statutory boards. File applications by the published deadlines.
- HQMC MMOA. Owns the schedule.
- S-1 staff. Track OMPF and MBS posture for board-eligible officers.

## Action items

1. Pull individual solicitation MARADMINs for each board.
2. Confirm OMPF accuracy 12 months before convene date.
3. Confirm obligated service requirements through TFRS.
