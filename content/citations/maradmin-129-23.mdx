## Scope

MARADMIN 129/23 provided clarification to MARADMIN 051/23, Expansion of the Military Parental Leave Program. The MARADMIN refined eligibility, leave timing, and reporting rules. Superseded by MARADMIN 191/26 for Reserve Component leave.

## Audience

- Marines using parental leave. Apply the current MARADMIN 051/23 framework.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 051/23. Expansion of the Military Parental Leave Program. The base policy this MARADMIN clarifies.
- MARADMIN 191/26. RCML and RCPL Changes. Cancelled this MARADMIN for the Reserve Component framework.

## Status

Released 2023-03. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MP. Cancelled by MARADMIN 191/26 for Reserve Component framework.
