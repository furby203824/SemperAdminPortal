## Scope

MARADMIN 619/14 published the Hardship Duty Pay - Tempo (HDP-T) Program Details. The MARADMIN sets the original eligibility, rate, and payment processing rules referenced by successive HDP-T fiscal year MARADMINs. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines on qualifying high tempo operational rotations. Eligible for HDP-T.
- Disbursing officers. Process HDP-T payments.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD FMR Volume 7A. HDP-T rate framework.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 510/14. Authorization for Hardship Duty Pay - Tempo.
- MARADMIN 414/20. FY21 HDP-T Program Details. Subsequent year update.
- MARADMIN 092/21. Update to MARADMIN 414/20 FY21 HDP-T Program Details.

## Status

Released 2014-12. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MP. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.
