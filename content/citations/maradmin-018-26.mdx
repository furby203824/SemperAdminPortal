## Scope

MARADMIN 018/26 announces GenAI.mil as the Marine Corps enterprise generative artificial intelligence (GenAI) platform. The Department of War fielded GenAI.mil as the enterprise platform. The Marine Corps consolidates duplicative, general-purpose GenAI usage onto GenAI.mil to optimize resources and standardize the user experience. The MARADMIN supersedes MARADMIN 496/24. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines, Department of the Navy civilians, and contractors using government-furnished equipment. Authorized to use GenAI.mil immediately.
- Deputy Commandant for Information (DC I). Designated governance authority. Approves any separate GenAI capability proposal.
- Service Data Office (SDO). Owns the AI lead and service data officer roles.
- Commanders. Coordinate with DC I before procuring or fielding any separate GenAI capability.
- Information security officers. Verify users follow CUI handling rules.

## Platform availability

- Login URL https://genai.mil.
- Authorized for Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI).
- Barred from Personally Identifiable Information (PII), Protected Health Information (PHI), and classified information.
- Capabilities at release. Conversational chat. File upload (PDF, DOCX, TXT). Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG). Secure web grounding. Deep research. Persistent chat histories.
- Planned. Higher classification networks, Marine Corps data integration, agentic AI development, API access.

## User responsibility

- GenAI tools are non-deterministic. Output is allowed to be inaccurate, biased, or incomplete.
- Critically evaluate and verify every output before using it for official purposes.
- The user remains accountable for products derived from the system.

## Governance

- DC I is the designated authority over GenAI capabilities. Any command or organization developing, procuring, or fielding a separate GenAI capability coordinates with and receives DC I approval before initiation. Ensures interoperability, security, and no duplication.
- NIPRGPT is sunsetting. NIPRGPT is no longer available for use.
- Other authorized GenAI systems (example, CamoGPT) remain authorized for their intended and approved purpose where the system meets the requirements for use on government systems.

## Support

- Platform issues. osw.genai@mail.mil. Primary help desk for system errors and functionality questions.
- Feedback and general inquiries. osw.genai@mail.mil.
- Unresolved issues. Contact the MARADMIN POCs.
- Training resources. https://genai.mil/resources/training.
- FAQ. https://genai.mil/resources/faq.

## Action items

1. Brief every Marine and civilian on the GenAI.mil URL and the CUI handling rule.
2. Halt use of NIPRGPT.
3. Coordinate any separate GenAI capability proposal through DC I before procurement or fielding.
4. Confirm Marines verify GenAI output before applying it to official products.
