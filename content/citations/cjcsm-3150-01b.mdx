## Citation clarification

CJCSM 3150.01B is not an active Chairman's Joint Chiefs of Staff Manual publication. Authors and inspection items have referenced this number in error or against a superseded version. Current Joint Reporting Structure guidance lives in the CJCSM 3150 series under different specific manual numbers.

## Where the actual policy lives

- CJCSM 3150.03D. Joint Reporting Structure, Personnel. The active personnel-reporting manual in the 3150 series.
- CJCSM 3150 series. Active manuals covering joint reporting topics by subject (personnel, casualty, logistics, intelligence).
- CJCSI 3401.02B. Force Readiness Reporting. The companion instruction setting the policy framework.
- DODD 7730.65. Department of Defense Readiness Reporting System.
- Joint Publication 1-02. DoD Dictionary of Military and Associated Terms.

## How to fix authoring references

Replace any inline citation to CJCSM 3150.01B with the specific manual that governs the topic. For personnel reporting, cite CJCSM 3150.03D. For force readiness reporting, cite CJCSI 3401.02B. For casualty reporting, cite the relevant active CJCSM 3150 manual and MCO 3040.4.
