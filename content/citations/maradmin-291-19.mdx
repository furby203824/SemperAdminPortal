## Scope

MARADMIN 291/19 set early Command Climate Survey compliance requirements for the Marine Corps. Superseded by MARADMIN 468/23 framework and current DoDI 6400.11 governance. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Commanders required to conduct annual and change-of-command CCAs. Apply the current MARADMIN 468/23 framework.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- DoDI 6400.11. DoD Integrated Primary Prevention Policy.
- MARADMIN 468/23. Command Climate Assessment Policy Updates. Current framework (page reads CANCELLED).
- MCO 5354.1F. Marine Corps Equal Opportunity Manual.

Released 2019-05. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA. Superseded by MARADMIN 468/23 and subsequent CCA guidance.
