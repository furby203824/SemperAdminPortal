## Scope

MARADMIN 115/24 clarifies PCS allowances for Marines attending Professional Military Education (PME). The MARADMIN aligns travel and household goods entitlements with the Joint Travel Regulations. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines on orders to PME. Apply the PCS allowance framework.
- Disbursing officers and Defense Travel Administrators. Process PCS vouchers under this framework.

## Connection to higher policy

- Joint Travel Regulations.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 7220.56A. BAH at Locations Other Than PDS. Chapter 3 covers PCS for PME.

## Status

Released 2024-03. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MP. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.
