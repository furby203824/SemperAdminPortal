## Scope

MARADMIN 595/24 announces changes to the Fitness Report format for sergeant major and master gunnery sergeant under MCO 1610.7B Chapter 4 paragraph 2(b). Effective 01 January 2025, sergeant major and master gunnery sergeant fitness reports no longer use the page one, five, and appropriate addendum pages format. Reports submit in the five page format currently in use for sergeant through lieutenant colonel. The adjustment gives senior enlisted high visibility and general officer slates the most relevant performance information. Omission and extension criteria in MCO 1610.7B and MARADMIN 634/23 remain in effect. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Reporting Seniors (RS) and Reviewing Officers (RO) for sergeants major and master gunnery sergeants. Author the five page format effective 01 January 2025.
- Sergeants major and master gunnery sergeants. Receive the full five page format report.
- Senior enlisted high visibility slate members. Receive the richer performance picture.
- General officer slate members. Receive the richer performance picture for E9 talent management decisions.
- S-1 staff and admin (0102, 0111). Process the new five page format E9 FITREPs in MCTFS.

## Format change

- Old format. Pages one, five, and the appropriate addendum pages only.
- New format. The five page format currently used for sergeant through lieutenant colonel FITREPs.
- Effective 01 January 2025.
- Section by section content matches the five page sergeant through lieutenant colonel template.

## Interim guidance

The MARADMIN serves as interim guidance pending the next release of MCO 1610.7B. The next release will fold the change into the order. Fitness reports write and process under the current MCO 1610.7B and this MARADMIN.

## Action items

1. Brief every reporting senior and reviewing officer carrying a sergeant major or master gunnery sergeant on the new five page format.
2. Pull the current sergeant through lieutenant colonel FITREP template. Apply to E9 reports starting 01 January 2025.
3. Audit the MCTFS configuration. Confirm the five page format submits cleanly for E9 reports.
4. Apply MARADMIN 634/23 omission and extension criteria. 180 days for AN and AR. 89 days for SA.
