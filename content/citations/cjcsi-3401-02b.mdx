## Scope

CJCSI 3401.02B establishes uniform policy, procedures, and criteria for reporting authoritative information to the President and Secretary of Defense related to the readiness of military forces to meet missions and goals assigned by the Secretary of Defense. Issued 31 May 2011 by the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff. Directive current as of 17 July 2014. Applicability is the Joint Chiefs of Staff, the Joint Staff, combatant commands, the Services, and DoD Combat Support Agencies responsive to the Chairman (DIA, DISA, NGA, DLA, DCMA, NSA, DTRA, and subordinate agencies).

## Audience

- Joint Chiefs of Staff and the Joint Staff.
- Combatant commands. Submit readiness reports per the uniform system.
- The Services. Marine Corps reports through HQMC DC PP&O.
- Combat Support Agencies. DIA, DISA, NGA, DLA, DCMA, NSA, DTRA.
- Marine Corps DRRS-MC program managers and unit operations officers.
- Commanders preparing readiness inputs.

## Sections

- Paragraph 1 Purpose. Uniform readiness reporting to the President and SECDEF.
- Paragraph 2 Cancellation. Cancels CJCSI 3401.02A, 27 February 2004 (Global Status of Resources and Training System).
- Paragraph 3 Applicability. JCS, Joint Staff, COCOMs, Services, CSAs.
- Paragraph 4 Policy. Implements 10 USC Section 153 (3)(C) and (3)(D) Chairman advisory and uniform system authority.
- Paragraph 5 Definitions. Reference to Joint Publication 1-02.
- Paragraph 6 Responsibilities.
- Paragraph 7 Summary of Changes.
- Enclosures A through D.
