## Scope

DD Form 2701 delivers initial victim and witness rights information at the start of a military criminal investigation or prosecution. The form lists the statutory rights of a crime victim, identifies the points of contact at the command, legal office, and military attorney level, and captures the victim's election regarding legal representation. Prescribed by DoDI 1325.07. Updated 21 August 2024.

## Audience

- Special Victims' Counsel (SVC) and Victims' Legal Counsel (VLC). Provide DD 2701 to eligible victims at first contact.
- Victim Advocates and Sexual Assault Response Coordinators (SARC).
- Family Advocacy Program (FAP) staff.
- Military criminal investigators. NCIS, CID, OSI, or service-equivalent.
- Trial counsel and Staff Judge Advocate offices.
- Command staff. Receive copies for the case file.

## Key fields

- Overview of Victim Rights. Fairness, dignity, protection from accused, notice of hearings, presence at hearings, ability to be heard, confer with prosecutor, legal assistance, return of property, full and timely restitution, proceedings free from unreasonable delay, privacy.
- Contact information block. Victim Witness Liaison, criminal investigator, prosecutor, legal assistance attorney, SVC/VLC, state crime victim compensation office.
- Legal representation note for victims of sexual assault, related offenses, or domestic violence.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- DoDI 1325.07. DoD Confinement of Military Prisoners and Administration of Military Correctional Programs and Facilities. Prescribing authority for DD 2701.
- DoDI 1030.2 and DoDD 1030.1. Victim and Witness Assistance Procedures and Policy.
- Marine Corps Victim Witness Assistance Program (VWAP) per MCO 5800.16 Vol. 9.
- DD Form 2704. Companion certification for prisoner-status notification election.

## Status

Active. Filed at the start of every covered case. Maintained in the case file and the Victim Witness Liaison file.
