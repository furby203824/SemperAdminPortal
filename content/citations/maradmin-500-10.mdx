## Scope

MARADMIN 500/10 publishes Marine Corps policy on Invitational Travel Orders (ITO) for family members attending unit memorial services. The MARADMIN authorizes government-funded travel for next of kin to attend memorial services for fallen Marines. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Casualty Assistance Calls Officers (CACOs). Coordinate ITO issuance with the family.
- Commanding officers. Authorize ITO for unit memorial services.
- Disbursing offices and Defense Travel Administrators. Process ITO travel.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 3040.4. Marine Corps Casualty Assistance Program.
- Joint Travel Regulations. ITO authority.

## Status
