## Scope

MARADMIN 388/25 publishes Fiscal Year 2026 class dates for Cornerstone, the Commandant's Combined Commandership Course. Successor to MARADMIN 377/24 (FY25 dates). Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Board-selected commanders, CSELs, and spouses. Required attendance.
- Marine Corps University. Hosts the course at Quantico VA.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 377/24. FY25 Cornerstone Course Dates. Predecessor FY message.

## Status

Released 2025-08. Originator CG EDCOM PRES MCU Quantico VA. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.
