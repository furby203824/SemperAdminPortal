## Scope

MARADMIN 191/25 announces the FY26 Selective Retention Bonus (SRB) Program and FY26 Broken Service SRB (BSSRB) Program. Marines with ECC between 2025-10-01 and 2026-09-30 are eligible. Successive year is MARADMIN 023/26 (FY27 SRB).

## Audience

- Marines with FY26 ECC. Eligible for SRB.
- Career planners. Apply PMOS bonus tables and kicker options.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 7220.24P. Selective Retention Bonus Program.
- MARADMIN 023/26. FY27 SRB Program. Next-year version.
- MARADMIN 585/24. FY26 Enlisted Retention Campaign.

## Status

Released 2025-04. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MP. Applicability is the Active Component.
