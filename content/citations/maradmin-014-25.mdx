## Scope

MARADMIN 014/25 advertises FY25 lateral move opportunities available to active component Marines with an End of Current Contract (ECC) in FY25. Marines who have not submitted for reenlistment or whose PMOS is closed are encouraged to submit a lateral move request. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Active component Marines with FY25 ECC. Eligible to submit lateral move requests.
- Career planners. Apply NAVMC 1200.1K Military Occupational Specialties Manual requirements.
- Disbursing officers. Apply SRB bonus eligibility from MARADMIN 483/24.

## Action items

1. Pull NAVMC 1200.1K for PMOS unique requirements.
2. Coordinate with the unit Career Counselor for the lateral move process.
3. Apply MARADMIN 326/23 enlisted active duty service limits to confirm STAP TIG for promotion.
