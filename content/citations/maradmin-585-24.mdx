## Scope

MARADMIN 585/24 announces the Fiscal Year 2026 Enlisted Retention Campaign beginning 2025-01-01. The campaign covers Marines with End of Current Contract (ECC) between 2025-10-01 and 2026-09-30 (FY26 Retention Cohort). Reenlistment channels include Commandant's Retention Program (CRP), standard reenlistments, lateral moves (LATMOVs), Commanding General's Retention Program (CGRP), and Direct Affiliation Program (DAP). Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Marines in the FY26 Retention Cohort. Eligible to reenlist via the listed channels.
- Career planners. Apply submission timelines and channel guidelines.
- HQMC MMEA. Owns the campaign. Releases per-PMOS guidance via TFRS.
- Major Subordinate Commands. Request monitor visits no later than 90 days prior. Primary window 2025-08-01 to 2025-12-15.

## Submission timeline

- 2025-01-01. Commandant's Retention Program (CRP). Targeted FTAP LATMOVs (0211, 0321, 0372, 1751, 2336, 3044, 4133, 5821, 7316). SDA and Orders.
- 2025-04-01. First Term Alignment Plan (FTAP) and all FTAP LATMOVs.
- 2025-06-01. Subsequent Term Alignment Plan (STAP), STAP LATMOVs, Senior Enlisted Alignment Plan (SEAP).

## CRP framework

- Selects up to 20 percent of a PMOS population based on historical retention performance and Service need.
- Selection driven by Junior Enlisted Performance Evaluation System (JEPES) retention scores.
- Selectees announced via MARADMIN before 2025-01-01.
- Enhanced Duty Station Incentive. Selectees pick an MCC assignment ahead of peers.
- No deadline. Selectees accept reenlistment up until ECC subject to boatspace.

## Local Command Authority extensions

Commands authorized to approve LCA extensions beginning 2025-01-01. Marines extend up to 2026-08 to facilitate reenlistments under this campaign. MARADMIN 527/25 Change 1 revised the local extension to 2026-05.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 1040.31. Enlisted Retention and Career Development Program.
- MARADMIN 199/25. FY26 Command Retention Mission. Companion MSC mission framework.
- MARADMIN 527/25. Change 1 to FY26 Enlisted Retention Campaign. Updates this campaign.
- MARADMIN 191/25. FY26 SRB Program and BSSRB.
- Total Force Retention System (TFRS). System of record.
