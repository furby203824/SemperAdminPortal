## Scope

MARADMIN 349/23 discontinued the discounted lump sum Assignment Incentive Pay (AIP) option. The MARADMIN simplified AIP processing and reduced administrative burden. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Marines on Special Duty Assignment receiving AIP. Apply the simplified rate structure.
- Disbursing officers. Process AIP under the new framework.
- HQMC MPO. Owns the policy.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 7220.12R. Special Duty Assignment Pay, Assignment Incentive Pay, and Volunteer Supplemental Incentive.
- MARADMIN 181/26. FY28 SDA Campaign. Current SDA Pay framework.
- MARADMIN 628/20. FY21 SDAP/AIP/VSI rates.

## Status

Released 2023-06. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MP. Applicability is the Active Component.
