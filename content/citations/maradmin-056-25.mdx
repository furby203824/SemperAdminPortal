## Scope

MARADMIN 056/25 announces the release of NAVMC 5239.1, USMC Guidance on Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI), dated 2024-12-04. The guidance covers development, deployment, and use of GenAI within the Marine Corps. Commanders and senior leaders should advocate for GenAI use in appropriate use cases. Use evaluates and monitors under government information system policies. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Generative AI system developers, system owners, and users. Apply the NAVMC 5239.1 framework.
- Commanders and senior leaders. Advocate for GenAI use in appropriate use cases.
- DC I Service Data Office. Owns AI lead for the Marine Corps.

## Key points

- GenAI tools present unique challenges in data privacy, security, and content control.
- GenAI systems produce misleading, inaccurate, and ungrounded information.
- The guidance establishes expectations for ethical and responsible use.
- Guidance location. MCPEL at https://www.marines.mil/News/Publications/MCPEL/Electronic-Library-Display/Article/4013464/navmc-52391/.
