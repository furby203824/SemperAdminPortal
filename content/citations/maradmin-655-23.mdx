## Scope

MARADMIN 655/23 announces full operational capability of the Marine Corps Office of Special Trial Counsel (OSTC) effective 2023-12-28. Under Rule for Courts-Martial 301 as amended by Annex 2 of Executive Order 14103, commanders shall ensure every allegation of a covered offense is promptly reported to the OSTC. OSTC holds exclusive authority over covered offenses committed on or after 2023-12-28 and is the exclusive referral authority. Commanders cannot dispose of an offense over which OSTC has exercised authority unless a Special Trial Counsel (STC) defers the offense. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Commanders. Report every allegation reasonably alleging a covered offense to the regional OSTC office.
- Staff Judge Advocates. Adjudicate implementation questions on the FY22, FY23, and FY24 NDAA military justice reforms.
- HQMC SJA. Owns the policy.
- OSTC offices (regional). Receive reports and adjudicate authority over the alleged offense.

## Covered offenses under the UCMJ

- Article 117a. Wrongful Broadcast or Distribution of Intimate Visual Images.
- Article 118. Murder.
- Article 119. Manslaughter.
- Article 119a. Death or Injury of an Unborn Child (pre-2023-12-28 cases excluded).
- Article 120. Rape and Sexual Assault.
- Article 120a. Mail, Deposit of Obscene Matter (pre-2023-12-28 cases excluded).
- Article 120b. Rape and Sexual Assault of a Child.
- Article 120c. Other Sexual Misconduct.
- Article 125. Kidnapping.
- Article 128b. Domestic Violence.
- Article 130. Stalking.
- Article 132. Retaliation.
- Article 134. Child Pornography.
- Conspiracy, solicitation, or attempt to commit a covered offense is itself a covered offense.

## Action items

1. Identify the OSTC regional office aligned to the unit location.
2. Report allegations promptly. The OSTC determines whether the offense is a covered offense.
3. Continue OPREP-3 Serious Incident Reports and law enforcement notifications.
4. Coordinate with the Staff Judge Advocate on disposition questions when OSTC has exercised authority.
