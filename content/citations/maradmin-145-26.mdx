## Scope

MARADMIN 145/26 announces the Academic Year 2026-2028 National Intelligence University (NIU) Reserve Component Masters Degree Programs. NIU at Bethesda MD offers accredited masters programs in intelligence-related fields tailored to Reserve Component members. Applicability is the Reserve Component.

## Audience

- Reserve component Marines interested in NIU masters degree programs. Eligible to apply.
- HQMC Reserve Affairs (RA). Adjudicates applications.
- Career planners and S-1 staff for Reserve Component. Track applications.

## Action items

1. Pull NIU program requirements from https://ni-u.edu.
2. Submit application through Reserve Affairs CMT.
3. Confirm IDT eligibility for participation.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 224/26. CY2026 SNCO Selection Boards. References this MARADMIN.

Released 2026-04. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA RA. Applicability is the Reserve Component.
