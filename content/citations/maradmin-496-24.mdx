## Scope

MARADMIN 496/24 announces two authorized conversational Artificial Intelligence (AI) Large Language Model (LLM) systems for use on government systems and with government data. CamoGPT runs on NIPR (IL5 including CUI) and SIPR (IL6). NIPRGPT runs on NIPR (IL5 including CUI). The capabilities are experimental and produce misleading, inaccurate, or incorrect results. Users remain responsible for human review of every output before acceptance. Superseded by MARADMIN 018/26 for the GenAI.mil enterprise platform.

## Audience

- Marines and civilians using government systems and data. Authorized to use CamoGPT and NIPRGPT.
- DC I Service Data Office. Owns the program.
- Information professionals. Apply the human-review responsibility to every output.

## CamoGPT

- Developed by the US Army AI Integration Center (AI2C).
- Deployment. NIPR (IL5 including CUI) and SIPR (IL6).
- Features. Persistent chat history. File upload (PDF, DOCX, TXT). Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG). Folders for chat organization. Preset prompts.
- Login. NIPRNet at https://www.omni.army.mil/camogpt. Commercial at https://omni.army.mil/camogpt.
- Account creation. https://forms.osi.apps.mil/r/WcarqEAYxL.
- Help desk. usarmy.camogpt@army.mil.
- Feedback. https://forms.osi.apps.mil/r/1WW3Q4dLSX.

## NIPRGPT

- Developed by Department of the Air Force and Air Force Research Lab.
- Deployment. NIPR (IL5 including CUI).
- Features. Persistent chat history. File upload (PDF, DOCX, TXT). RAG. Folders. Preset prompts.
- Registration. https://niprgpt.mil/. IL4 user spaces limited. Waitlist when slots are full.
- Help desk. afrl.ri.niprgpt@us.af.mil.
- Feedback. https://forms.osi.apps.mil/r/0WncKswckv.

## Use cases

- Assist in writing awards.
- Summarize reports or papers.
- Draft weekly situation reports.
- Compose complex emails.
- Elevate written products (policy development).
- Code within a secure computing environment.
- Experimentation with generative AI safely.
