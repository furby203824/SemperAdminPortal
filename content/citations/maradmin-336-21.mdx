## Scope

MARADMIN 336/21 provides amplifying guidance on the Defense Travel System (DTS) Unsubmitted Travel Voucher Management. The MARADMIN supports voucher submission compliance and command reporting. Superseded by MARADMIN 359/25, the current Unsubmitted DTS Travel Voucher and Outstanding Travel Order framework.

## Audience

- Defense Travel Administrators. Apply current MARADMIN 359/25 framework.
- Travelers. Submit vouchers within 5 working days.
- Finance and Disbursing offices. Reconcile unsubmitted vouchers.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 4650.39A. Marine Corps Defense Travel System (DTS) Order.
- MARADMIN 359/25. Updated Guidance for Processing Unsubmitted DTS Vouchers. Current framework.
