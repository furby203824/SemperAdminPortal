## Scope

MARADMIN 574/24 publishes the implementation details for transport or shipment reimbursement of human breast milk expenses during a Permanent Change of Station (PCS). Eligible Service Members receive up to $1,000 reimbursement. The MARADMIN implements Section 626 of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2023. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Service Members who have given birth within 12 months and are executing PCS. Eligible.
- Disbursing officers. Process reimbursement claims.

## Connection to higher policy

- Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2023 (Public Law 117-328) Section 626.
- Joint Travel Regulations.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 573/24. TCOP Initiatives Overview. Companion family support framework summarizing the breast milk reimbursement.
- MARADMIN 537/24. Travel Reimbursement for Designated Childcare Provider. Companion family support.
