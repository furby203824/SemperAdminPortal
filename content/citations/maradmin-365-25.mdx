## Scope

MARADMIN 365/25 advertises FY26 lateral move opportunities for active component Marines with an End of Current Contract (ECC) in FY26. Update to MARADMIN 014/25 (FY25 LATMOV). Marines who have not submitted for reenlistment or whose PMOS is closed are encouraged to submit a lateral move request. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Active component Marines with FY26 ECC. Eligible to submit LATMOV requests.
- Career planners. Apply NAVMC 1200.1K PMOS requirements. Confirm boatspace and school seat availability.

## Available PMOS opportunities

FTAP and STAP eligibility.

- 0211 CI/HUMINT Specialist, 0321 Reconnaissance Marine, 0372 Critical Skills Operator, 1751 Influence Specialist, 1834 ACV Crewmember, 2143 ACV Repairer/Technician, 2336 EOD Technician, 5955 Ground Control Station Technician, 5974 Tactical Data Systems Technician, 5979 Tactical Air Operations/Air Defense Systems Technician, 6214 UAS Mechanic MQ-9, 6314 Avionics/Maintenance Technician UAS.

FTAP only. 3044 Operational Contract Support, 4133 MCCS Marine, 4821 Career Counselor, 5821 Criminal Investigator Agent, 7212 LAAD Gunner.

STAP only. 0681 Information Security Technician, 1721 Cyberspace Warfare Operator, 8412 Career Recruiter.
