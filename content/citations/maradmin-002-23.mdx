## Scope

MARADMIN 002/23 cancels six COVID-19-related MARADMINs from 2020 through 2022. The cancellation removes the cancelled MARADMINs from active reference and routes commanders to current force health protection guidance. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Commanders, S-1 staff, and medical providers. Discontinue reference to the six cancelled MARADMINs.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 754/20. COVID-19 Vaccine Guidance for Active and Reserve Components.
- MARADMIN 462/21, 533/21, 612/21, 733/21, 464/22. Cancelled COVID-19 framework updates.

## Status

Released 2023-01. Originator CMC Washington DC. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.
