## Scope

MARADMIN 150/22 announces an advance change to MCO 7220.56A, Entitlement to BAH at Locations Other Than PDS. The MARADMIN updates eligibility criteria and waiver processing for current BAH waivers. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force beyond entry-level training.

## Audience

- Marines submitting BAH waiver requests. Apply updated criteria.
- HQMC MMIB-3. Adjudicates waiver requests.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 7220.56A. BAH at Locations Other Than PDS. The order receiving this change.
- MARADMIN 126/14. Manpower Management Division (MM) Reorganization. Defines MMIB-3 as the Secretarial Process authority.

## Status

Released 2022-03. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MP. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force beyond entry-level training.
