## Scope

MARADMIN 229/25 announces noncompetitive Corporal to Sergeant promotion procedure for Reserve Component Recruiters. Companion to MARADMIN 103/25 for the Active Component. Applicability is the Marine Corps Reserve.

## Audience

- Reserve Corporals on Recruiter duty. Eligible for noncompetitive promotion.
- Reserve career planners. Process the promotion warrant.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P1400.32D. Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions.
- MARADMIN 103/25. Noncompetitive AD Corporal to Sergeant Recruiter Promotions. Active Component companion.

Released 2025-05. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MM. Applicability is the Marine Corps Reserve.
