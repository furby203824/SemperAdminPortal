## Scope

MARADMIN 302/17 announced advance notification of changes to MCO 7220.12R Special Duty Assignment Pay (SDAP) Program. The MARADMIN announced restructuring of SDAP rates and authorization framework. Successive MARADMINs (628/20, 438/19, 181/26) revised the rate tables. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Marines on Special Duty Assignment (Recruiter, Drill Instructor, MSG, Combat Instructor). Apply current SDAP rates.
- Career planners and disbursing officers. Apply the rate framework.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 7220.12R. Special Duty Assignment Pay Program. Parent policy.
- MARADMIN 628/20. FY21 SDAP/AIP/VSI rates. Current rate framework.
- MARADMIN 438/19. FY21 SDA and Screenable Billet Incentives.
- MARADMIN 181/26. FY28 SDA Campaign.

## Status

Released 2017-06. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MP. Superseded by MARADMIN 628/20 and subsequent SDAP rate MARADMINs. Use this entry for historical context.
