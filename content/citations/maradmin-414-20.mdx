## Scope

MARADMIN 414/20 reauthorizes the Hardship Duty Pay - Tempo (HDP-T) program for the Marine Corps. The Assistant Secretary of the Navy for Manpower and Reserve Affairs reauthorized the DON HDP-T program for a three year period through 2023-09-30. The MARADMIN cancels MARADMIN 538/19, the prior FY20 program details. MARADMIN 510/14 and MARADMIN 619/14 remain in effect for the program rules. The source page reads CANCELLED.

## Audience

- Active component Marines. Eligible for HDP-T payments under the program rules.
- Reserve component Marines. Eligible for HDP-T payments under the program rules.
- Pay and disbursing offices. Process HDP-T payments through 2023-09-30.
- Commanders. Confirm Marines eligible for HDP-T receive payment under the prior MARADMIN guidance.

## Policy

- Effective 2020-10-01. HDP-T payments reauthorized through 2023-09-30.
- All other guidance in MARADMIN 510/14 and MARADMIN 619/14 stays in effect.
- MARADMIN 538/19 (FY20 details) is cancelled.

## Action items

1. Confirm Marines in qualifying status receive HDP-T payment effective 2020-10-01.
2. Apply the program rules from MARADMIN 510/14 and MARADMIN 619/14.
3. Coordinate with pay and disbursing offices for any past-due payments.
4. Pull the current HDP-T reauthorization MARADMIN since the source page reads CANCELLED on this entry.

## Connection to higher policy

- Assistant Secretary of the Navy (Manpower and Reserve Affairs) Memorandum dated 2020-07-01. Authorizes payment of HDP-T for three years.
